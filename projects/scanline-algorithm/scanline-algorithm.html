Rasterization

In the Rasterization Stage, the main goal is the convertion of primitives to an image of pixels. There are multiple ways to approximate a shape and translate it to an realistic pixel image. Here we are discussing the procedure of midline algorithm. This algorithm can be implemented for multiple shapes. In this example we will discuss the shapes of a line and a circle.
