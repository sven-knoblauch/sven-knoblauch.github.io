One Dimensional Perlin Noise

Perlin Noise is noise where there are no jumps on adjecent values. This is visible in the more smooth look of the noise and the absence of hard edges. To calculate this noise, a simple array with random numbers are calculated and the the values between the array are interpolated, for example with a polynomial of degree 5.
